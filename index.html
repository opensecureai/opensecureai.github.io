Our Principles

Foundational principles that guide all our AI development

Human-Centered AI

AI systems must empower humans with tools that enhance their capabilities while preserving human agency, dignity, and privacy rights above all else.

Interpretability & Control

AI systems provide clear explanations for their decisions and give users meaningful control over how they operate, ensuring humans can understand, guide, and override AI behavior when needed.

Globally Legal

Our AI systems comply with local laws and regulations across all jurisdictions, ensuring responsible deployment that respects regional requirements while maintaining global reach and accessibility.

IT Security

Provide comprehensive tech security controls integrated with the well established domain specific regulation requirements, (e.g. safety, compliance).

Non Violence Ethical

Refusal to cooperate with organizations associated with violence practices including military, terrorist, or any entities engaged in violent activities.

Well-being Focus

Channeling AI productivity gains into enhanced human well-being and life balance, ensuring technology serves to improve quality of life rather than increase workloads or stress.
